Dr. Jiban Podder

Professor

Jiban Podder is a Professor of Physics, Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), Dhaka, Bangladesh where he has been teaching since 1st March 1989. Podder was born in Narayangang, Bangladesh on 24th December 1959. He received his B.Sc. (Honours) and M.Sc. in Physics from Dhaka University in 1980 and 1981 respectively. Podder received his M.Phil in 1987 from BUET and Ph.D in Physics from BUET in 1995 under the research collaboration of Anna University, India. Podder joined at the Bangladesh Atomic Energy Commission as a Scientific Officer in June 1987. He was appointed as a lecturer in the Department of Physics, BUET in 1989. He became an Assistant Professor in 1992, Associate Professor in 1998 and full Professor in 2002. Dr. Podder pursued Postdoctoral research at Anna University, India, during 2000-2001 and Nagoya Institute of Technology, Japan under JSPS fellowship during 2003-2005. Prof. Podder served the Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering, University of Saskatchewan, Canada as Visiting Professor from 2012 to 2015. Dr. Podder’s area of research interests include growth kinetics, Non-linear optical crystals, compound semiconductor thin films, nano-materials, carbon and carbonaceous materials for optoelectronic and photonic applications.

Dr. Podder has supervised so far 20 M.Sc, 30 M. Phil. and 6 Ph.D students. Prof. Podder has evaluated over 250 Ph.D theses from different Universities around the world. As of 2024, Prof. Podder has published over 195 research articles in refereed journals & proceedings and author of ‘Applied Engineering Physics: Electricity and Magnetism’ for Science and Engineering students and one U. S Patent Apl. No. 61/932662‘Methods and Apparatus for Crystallization of Salts’.Prof. Podder is a Fellow of Bangladesh Academy of Sciences, Bangladesh Physical Society and now acting as an Editor-In-Chief of the Bangladesh Journal of Scientific Research. Currently, Prof. Podder is working as a Visiting Professor in the Department of Chemical and Biological Engineering, University of Saskatchewan, Canada on sabbatical from October'2021 to September'2022. Prof. Podder was acting as Dean of the Faculty of Science, Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology from November 18, 2022 to November 17, 2024.
